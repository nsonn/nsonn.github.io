Travel

Laramie is located on Interstate 80 in southeastern Wyoming. Home to the University of Wyoming is 65 miles north of Fort Collins, Colorado.

The local airport is Laramie Regional Airport. The community's hotels (plenty of decent options!) are concentrated along the interstate corridor.

Things to Do

UW Art Museum

A small but well-curated art museum on the university campus.

Curt Gowdy State Park

An outdoorsy place.

Vedauwoo

Lots of big rocks.

Snowy Range

A scenic drive on Highway 130 through forests and mountains.

Sweet Melissa’s

Our favorite Laramie restaurant, with great vegetarian options.

A great way to learn about some unique Wyoming history.
